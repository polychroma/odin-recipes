Vodka Martini

Vodka replaces gin in this variation of the classic martini.

Ingredients:

  • 3 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce dry vermouth
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 olives

Steps:

  1. Combine vodka and dry vermouth in a cocktail mixing glass.
  2. Fill with ice and stir until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish with three olives on a toothpick.
