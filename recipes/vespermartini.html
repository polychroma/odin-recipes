Vesper Martini

This is the version of the martini that Bond orders in Casino Royale.

Ingredients:

  • Â¼ cup gin
  • 1 fluid ounce Russian vodka
  • 1 tablespoon Lillet blanc
  • ice cubes
  • 1 lemon twist

Steps:

  1. Pour gin, vodka, and Lillet over ice.
  2. Shake or stir (remember, Bond doesn't give a damn anymore!)
  3. Strain into a martini glass.
  4. Garnish with lemon twist.
